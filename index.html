BitMine

What is BitMine?

Bitmine is a website where it helps inform people about the finance world. This includes topics such as Bitcoin , Stocks, Bonds, Interest Rate and how it can impact you. On our website we can give people detailed descriptions on how to use bitcoin and even other things like finance, stocks, and even blockchain. Our website is meant to educate others as well as to act as a portal to other resources.

Why we created BitMine

After learning about how blockchain and Bitcoin function we realised that many black and brown folks don't learn about this information hence allowing for the economic gap to increase. Our aim is to educate everyone about how Bitcoin, Bitcoin mining, Blockchain and other aspects of financial literacy and help allow for the gap to be bridged.

What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a type of digital currency in which a record of transactions is maintained and new units of currency are generated by the computational solution of mathematical problems, and which operates independently of a central bank.
